The Change of Season Effect On Arrest Rates

A decrease in arrest rates could be on the horizon for New York City, studies show.

By: Nia Freeman | Nov 12, 2021

Background photo created by kjpargeter - www.freepik.com

As New Yorkers enter another winter season, police can prepare to make less arrest in the next couple of months. Studies show that arrest rates drop during the month of November and December. According to NYC Open Data, 19 percent more people were arrested in Ocotober than in November and December combined between the years 2015 and 2020.
